# Notes on the Blog Redesign

## Introduction

I suddenly felt like changing the style of this blog, so I went ahead and redesigned it. This post is just a short record of that change.

There is not a huge amount of content on this site yet, because for a long time I wrote more on my WeChat public account. Going forward, I want to keep writing here more consistently as well.

## Thoughts

The previous version of the blog felt like it lacked personality, so a redesign made sense.

Lately I have been surrounded by AI tools so much that I found myself wanting to use AI for everything, including writing articles. But the more I thought about it, the more I felt that a blog should remain a place for real thoughts and real experience, not a pile of polished but hollow AI-generated phrases.

So I decided to return to the original idea: I will not use AI to generate the articles themselves. This space should keep recording my own genuine thoughts and observations. I want to preserve a small clean corner for that.
