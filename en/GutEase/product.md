# Product Overview

**GutEase** is an iOS app for **food and digestion journaling**. It helps you record **what you ate** and **how your body felt** with less friction, so you can review patterns over time.

## What you can do with it

- **Meal logging**  
  Record meals by **taking photos** or **typing manually**. Multiple food items from a single meal can be organized and saved into the timeline.

- **Bathroom activity and comfort tracking**  
  Record bathroom activity, comfort level, and notes, then place them **on the same day-level timeline** as meal records for easier comparison.

- **Insights and summaries**  
  If supported in the current version, the app may provide **lightweight trend summaries or heuristic hints** based on your saved records. These are **not medical diagnosis or treatment advice**.

- **Food library**  
  If supported in the current version, you can maintain a personal food library to make future record-keeping faster.

## Who it is for

GutEase is designed for everyday users who want to **track food intake and digestive reactions consistently** and observe possible **diet-related patterns**.

**If you already have a diagnosed medical condition, are under treatment, or are taking medication, please follow professional medical advice. This app does not replace a doctor or professional care.**

## Notes about photo-based recognition

Some versions may include a feature that helps organize food information from photos. That feature may depend on **network access and third-party AI services**.

You can review the relevant data-transfer scope in the [Privacy Policy](/en/GutEase/privacy). If you do not want to use that feature, you can still keep records completely by hand.
